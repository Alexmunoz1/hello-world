How TS got started!

Call Of Duty Trickshoting started way back In COD 4, where there was bounces and not much to do as the games kept coming out people started to find creative shots and then the trickshotting started to get bigger when Bo2 came out as there was a lot to do in that game. Many people started to creat clans and many of them became big like Faze clan one of the first trickshotting clans out there. Faze caln got big off Mw2 or Modern Warfare 2, their standards back then are not the same anymore to get in you kind of have to be a content creator because of many kids playing and doing the same things the other people are doing like feeding and trickshotting you kinda want to be creative in order to get their attention.
